  - the bookshelves -

Used. New. Fiction. Nonfiction. Fairytales. Memoirs. Vampires. Psychological thrillers. We’ve got it all right here. Check out a bit about our store policies below:

Used & New Books:

Here at Loose Leaf, we prioritize sustainability. Therefore, a majority of our collection is used books. ← sold at reduced price (democratize knowledge!)

That being said, we fully recognize great books continue to be written, so we try to stock the top tier new releases to keep with the times. ← Our readers keep us young ♥

Trade-in policy:

Running out of space on your bookshelf? We’re here to help. Bring in your old books and earn points toward a new book! ← Okay , so maybe this won’t help with your shelf space, but let’s focus on the bottom line here: you get new books at a discount.

Author Events:

What’s better than buying a book? Buying a signed book after getting to chat with the author!

[Currently, we don’t have any events scheduled for the upcoming month. But be sure to check back later to see who we’re hosting next!]

  - the underGROUNDS-

Yes, we’re a bookstore and a café, but after hours, we undergo another transformation... ← sort of like a werewolf, but with less teeth

The Tea:

Just between you and us, Loose Leaf gets kind of lonely after closing. So, for its own good, after hours it becomes a rentable hot spot for book clubs, advocacy group meetings, and more! Have a meeting, but no meeting space? We’re here to help.

Just email looseleaf@fake.email with what you have in mind, and we’ll get back to you with more details. (We can’t give all our secrets away on here...)
